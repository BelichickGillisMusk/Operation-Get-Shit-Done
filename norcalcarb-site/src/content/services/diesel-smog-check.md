## What the Inspection Covers

California CARB regulations require annual emissions testing for diesel trucks over 14,000 lbs GVWR. Our mobile inspection tests:

- **Opacity (smoke)** — measured with a calibrated opacity meter
- **Idle emissions** — CO, HC, NOx levels at idle
- **OBD-II scan** — fault code check on applicable model years
- **Visual inspection** — EGR, DPF, and emissions control equipment

A passing vehicle receives a CARB-compliant smog certificate issued same day.

## Who Needs a Diesel Smog Check

Under the [CARB Truck and Bus Regulation](https://ww2.arb.ca.gov/our-work/programs/truck-and-bus-regulation), the following require annual testing:

- Diesel trucks 14,001 lbs GVWR and above, MY 2000+
- Diesel-powered buses and passenger vehicles
- Agricultural trucks (exemptions may apply — contact us)
- Fleets registered in California operating on CA roads

## We Are Inspection Only

NorCal CARB Mobile performs smog inspection and certification. **We do not perform mechanical repairs.** If a vehicle fails, we provide a detailed report and you arrange repairs with your diesel mechanic. We offer reduced-rate retests once repairs are completed.
