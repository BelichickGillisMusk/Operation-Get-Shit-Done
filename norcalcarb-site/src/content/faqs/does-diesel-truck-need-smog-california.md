---
question: "Does my diesel truck need a smog check in California?"
category: "compliance"
order: 1
---

Yes. California's CARB Truck and Bus Regulation requires annual smog testing for diesel trucks over 14,000 lbs GVWR, model year 2000 and newer, that operate on California roads. This applies to owner-operators, trucking companies, and any business with a commercial diesel fleet — regardless of whether the trucks are registered in California.

Exemptions may apply to some agricultural vehicles and pre-2000 model year trucks. Contact NorCal CARB Mobile at 916-890-4427 to confirm requirements for your specific vehicles.
